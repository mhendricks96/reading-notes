The Four Things All Computers Do

Take Input

The input is what comes from outside the computer. This can come from basocally anywhere.

Store Informaition

All information is converted into binary for storage. Binary code is basically a series of on/off swithces that are represented by 1's and 0's

Process Information

Information is processed with circuits. Each circuit carries one byte of information and is carried by a wire, the computer uses tons of tiny little binary processes and combine those together to achieve a result and then store that result. This is done over and over again until the information is ready to be output.

Output

This is what the copmuter sends back to the user. Once again, this can be just about anything like a screen.
